Few Words About Me

Hi, I'm Rafael Soufer an Uruguayan Front-End Developer and Team Leader at YAMA Group. On the other hand, I have a bachelor's in Graphic design and experience as a project manager.

I'm currently working as Lead for digital and web app projects at YAMA Group.

What is more, previously working as a web designer/developer at Publicis Impetu, has given me the opportunity to work with, very important clients such as Movistar, Nestlé, L'Oréal, and many more. That being said, I have not only accomplished my duties as a designer, but I also have experience in project management.
